ELECTION COMMISSION

IIT KANPUR

The election commission is mandated to foster the democratic spirit in the students’ self-governance set up - the Students’ Gymkhana. For upholding this spirit, it is necessary that the representatives of the students are elected by the process of free and fair elections.

Choose Candidate Wisely!

Look! How important your vote is.

  • Voting in student elections allows you to influence decisions that directly affect your daily life on campus, including policies on housing, dining, and academic resources.
  • Voting gives you the power to voice your opinions and concerns on issues that matter to you, ensuring that your needs and perspectives are heard and addressed.
  • Electing strong student leaders means you have advocates who will work on your behalf to improve campus life and address student issues effectively.
  • High voter turnout demonstrates a united student body, which can lead to more significant influence over university decisions and policies.
  • Participating in the voting process helps you understand the importance of leadership and governance, skills that are valuable both during and after your university years.
  • Voting holds student leaders accountable, ensuring they fulfill their promises and work diligently to represent their peers' interests.
