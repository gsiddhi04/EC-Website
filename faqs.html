Frequently Asked Questions

What is Election Commission?

The Election Commission is responsible for organizing and overseeing student elections, ensuring they are conducted fairly and transparently.

How often are student elections held?

Student elections are typically held annually in the spring semester.

Who is eligible to vote in student elections?

All the GBMs(General Body Member) i.e, currently enrolled students at IIT Kanpur are eligible to vote in student elections.

How can I run for a position in the student government?

To run for a position, you must complete a candidacy application form, meet the eligibility criteria, and gather a specified number of signatures from fellow students.

What are the eligibility criteria to run for office?

Eligibility criteria typically include being a current student in good academic standing and not being on academic probation or disciplinary action.

When is the deadline to submit candidacy applications?

The deadline for candidacy applications is usually announced at the beginning of the election cycle. Please check the Election Commission's schedule for specific dates.

How do I vote in the student elections?

Voting is conducted online through the IIT Kanpur's secure voting platform. Instructions and links to the voting platform will be provided via email.

How is the voting process secured?

EC uses a secure online voting platform with unique login credentials for each student to ensure the integrity and confidentiality of the voting process.

Are there rules for campaigning?

Yes, there are specific rules and guidelines for campaigning to ensure fairness. These include regulations on campaign materials, social media usage, and conduct during the campaign period. For further knowledge read CoC.

Where can I find the campaigning rules?

The campaigning rules and guidelines are available on the IITK Election Commission's website.

Can I campaign in student residences and dining halls?

Campaigning in certain locations like student residences and dining halls may have specific restrictions. Please refer to the campaigning guidelines for detailed information.

When will the election results be announced?

Election results are typically announced within 24-48 hours after the voting period ends. The results will be posted on the IITK Election Commission's website and shared via email.

What should I do if I suspect any irregularities or violations during the elections?

If you suspect any irregularities or violations, please report them to the Election Commission immediately. All reports will be investigated thoroughly.

Who should I contact for more information?

For more information, you can contact the Election Commission via email at email or visit the office during business hours.
